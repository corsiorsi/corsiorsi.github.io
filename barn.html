Our Barn- Holly Hill Show Stable

We ride out of the Hanover location of Holly Hill Show Stable. The barn is about 30 minutes outside of Boston. Team members drive over to lessons together, so no one is ever without a ride to the barn. Holly Hill is a hunter/jumper oriented barn that has a beautiful indoor ring, so we can ride even in the dead of winter! Our head trainer there is Phyllis Cervelli.
