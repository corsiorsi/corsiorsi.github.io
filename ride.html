FAQ

How do I join the team?

Easy! Come to our team tryouts at the beginning of each semester, and fill out this form! Or, email the team captain at lealam@bu.edu and we can set it up!

Do I have to already know how to ride to join?

Not at all! Most team members do have equestrian experience, but newcomers to the world of riding are always welcome. We have lessons for varying levels of ability

I don't have a car. How will I get to the barn?

Team members take club sports vans to the barn or drive each other if they have cars. So if you don't have your car at BU, you can still get to the barn!

Is this a varsity team?

We are a club sports team, not a varsity team
