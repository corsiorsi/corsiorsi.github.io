The Boston University Equestrian Team is a competitive club that competes in Zone 1, Region 4, of the Intercollegiate Horse Show Association (IHSA). Competition includes Hunter Seat Equitation on the Flat and Hunter Seat Equitation Over Fences. The club encourages riders of all levels of experience and training to try out. Rider divisions range from beginner (those who have had little to no experience riding) to advanced (those that compete at top shows and have participated in Medal finals). The team rides out of Holly Hill Show Stable in Hanover, MA under head coach Phyllis Cervelli.
